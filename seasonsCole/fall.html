The Fall Information Page

Autumn is another word used for Fall. Fall or Autumn is one of the four seasons. The four seasons are Winter, Spring, Summer, and Fall. Fall is the season between Summer and Winter. The first day of Autumn is at the end of September. The unofficial end of the summer season and the beginning of the Fall season is when the children go back to school. Colors associated with the season are red, yellow, orange, brown, and black
