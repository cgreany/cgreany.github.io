What is the actual Blacklist?

The Blacklist, is a list of criminals provided to a special undercover FBI unit by an informant. Essentially the Blacklist is a really long list of criminals that carry out massive underground terrorist acts.

Where does Reddington get people on Blacklist

There are several different ways Reddington potentially get the blacklist, but it's more than likely his intuition within the crinimal field which gives him information about the Blacklist and these criminals.
