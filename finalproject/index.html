What is the Blacklist?

The Blacklist is a NBC television show that is on Netflix. Which includes an informant that provides the FBI with a Blacklist. A blacklist is a list of people, organizations, or countries that are being shunned or excluded by others because they are alleged to have engaged in behavior or activities that are deemed unacceptable or unethical.
