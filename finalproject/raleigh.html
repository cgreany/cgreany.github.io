Who is Raleigh Sinclair III?

Raleigh Sinclair III was an obsessive-compulsive man that created perfect alibis for people who wanted to personally murder their victims, but needed a alibi that would allow them to avoid conviction.

Where does they rank on the Blacklist?

Raleigh Sinclair III was a interesting character in seasons 5 and ranked 51 on the Blacklist.

What did they do?

In total, Sinclair provided perfect alibis for 51 murders, including the deaths of a heir to a real estate empire and a Lebanese diplomat.
